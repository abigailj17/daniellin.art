Daniel Lin is a musician, music director, and activist. A talented cellist, pianist, guitarist, and singer, Daniel uses his music to explore the intersection between social justice and art. With a focus in improv jazz, Daniel has studied under and played with accomplished musicians including Bill Cole, Taylor Ho Bynum, and Tomeka Reid, and Althea SullyCole. Daniel has been the music director for youth productions at Northern Stage and the New London Barn Playhouse and is currently a Digital Library Fellow at Dartmouth College, where he works to preserve the history of activism on the Dartmouth campus. Above all, Daniel strives to create and sustain community through his art.
